About Eyog Dental

Eyog Dental Clinic is originated at Bagumbayan, Taguig City on September 2020. However, in order to give more quality services, the clinic had to relocate to a new location in front of Camp Bagong Diwa on October of the same year. Unfortunately, the clinic faced challenges when the COVID-19 pandemic emerged, leading to the temporary shutdown of its operations in June 2020. Despite the setback caused by the pandemic, Eyog Dental Clinic was determined to resume its services and continue serving the community. On March 13, 2021, the clinic successfully reestablished its operations in Siargao, providing dental care to the local residents and visitors to the island.

Mission

Our mission at Eyog Dental Clinic is to provide exceptional dental care and promote oral health while ensuring the comfort and satisfaction of our patients. We strive to deliver comprehensive, personalized treatment using the latest advancements in dental technology and techniques. Our dedicated team of professionals is committed to creating a warm and welcoming environment, where patients feel valued, respected, and confident in the quality of care they receive. We aim to improve the oral health and overall well-being of our patients, enhancing their smiles and positively impacting their lives.

Vision

Our vision at Eyog Dental Clinic is to be a leading provider of dental services, recognized for our excellence in patient care, innovation, and positive patient outcomes. We aspire to set the highest standards in dental practice by continuously advancing our knowledge, skills, and technologies. We aim to foster long-term relationships with our patients, built on trust, integrity, and open communication. Our vision includes being a center of dental excellence, where patients can receive a comprehensive range of services delivered with compassion, precision, and attention to detail. We envision a future where every individual enjoys optimal oral health, with confident smiles and improved overall quality of life.
